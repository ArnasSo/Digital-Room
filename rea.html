Rea Rossi - Contemporary Sculptor & Wearable Art Designer

Rea Rossi creates contemporary wearable sculptures and jewelry that give form to sound and vibration. She blends metals, digital fabrication, and craft to turn invisible sensory experiences into tangible art.

Born with bilateral hearing loss, Rossi channels her experience of hearing and hearing-devices into her practice. Her work references inner-ear cilia, resonance, and assistive technologies to transform impairment into aesthetic and expressive statements.
